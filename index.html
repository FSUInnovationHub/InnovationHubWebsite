Welcome to THE HUB!

Florida State University's new Innovation Hub is the result of a $2.5 million investment by the Provost to deepen FSU's distinctive commitment to continuous innovation. Located at the heart of FSU's campus, the Innovation Hub fills the first floor of the Louis Shores building.

Our Mission: To foster a collaborative community founded on a culture of creativity and innovation that identifies issues, explores opportunities, and develops solutions using design thinking and emerging technologies.

Our Vision: Students from all disciplines working together to combine their unique expertise and perspectives under the guidance of experienced mentors to create novel and effective solutions to the vexing, complex, and wicked problems of our day.

Our Values: We strongly believe that the healthiest, happiest, and most productive organizations embrace four essential values:

  • Love: Empathy / Compassion / Kindness / Fairness / Respect
  • Creativity: Curiosity / Inventiveness / Humor / Playfulness
  • Authenticity: Genuineness / Honesty / Integrity / Openness / Transparency
  • Grit: Tenacity / Commitment / Courage / Responsibility

Our Scope:Our primary focus is on undergraduate students from all backgrounds and majors, while serving as a resource for the entire FSU community, with outreach to K-12 students, strong connections to the Tallahassee/Leon County innovation ecosystem, and partnerships with businesses and corporations.

DESIGN THINKING @ THE HUB

Design Thinking is the core innovation framework used @ The Hub. Design Thinking, sometimes referred to as Human Centered Design, is an innovation framework rooted in empathy, strongly dependant on the benefits of diverse perspectives, with specially designed methods and tools for investigation, ideation, prototyping, and testing.

The hallmarks of Design Thinking are:

  • Focus on human values
  • Collaborate across boundaries
  • Seek diversity and equality
  • Mindfulness of process
  • Show don’t tell
  • Bias towards action (fail fast forward)
  • Iterate and prototype toward a solution

We offer an introductory course in Design Thinking open to all students - ENT3607, Innovation by Design. In this class groups of students take on major challenges using Design Thinking methods to develop ideal solutions. We also offer workshops, meetups and design sprints to bring together designers from across disciplines.

3D @ THE HUB

Design and prototype your 3D inventions in physical and virtual space.
Our Design Lab has high-powered graphics workstations for 3D design, and a materials library for low-fidelity prototyping.

The Digital Fablab

Our Digital Fablab has equipment for bringing your inventions to life. Our 3D printer farm harnesses the power of 30 networked 3D printers supporting a wide variety of materials and dimensions. We also have 3D scanners, laser cutters, Arduino boards, Raspberry Pi boards, Little bits, and a soldering station with a wide assortment of electronic components to create your next electronic creation.

The VR Lab

Our VR lab provides limitless opportunities for students to reimagine the world in virtual and augmented reality. With three Oculus Rifts, three HTC Vives, Mixed Reality headsets and a Microsoft Hololense AR headset, students can explore the most popular VR and AR platforms. Workshops and meetups bring a wide community of VR enthusiasts together to share knowledge and ideas.
