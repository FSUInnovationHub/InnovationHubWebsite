Transdisciplinary Discovery, Design, and Innovation

to confront the vexing, complex and wicked challenges of our day.

Florida State University's new Innovation Hub is the result of a $2.5 million investment from the Provost to deepen FSU's distinctive commitment to continuous innovation. Located at the heart of FSU's campus, the Innovation Hub is located with the School of Information in the Louis Shores building.

The 14,000 square foot space is designed to support Design Thinking with the latest technologies, a Digital Fablab, Virtual Reality Lab, Hackerspace, and a variety of spaces and technologies for collaborative work supporting groups from 2 to 140.

The Innovation Hub is currently open during the weekdays Monday - Friday from 9:00am - 6:00pm, with extended hours coming in mid to late March.

Transdisciplinary Partners

The Innovation Hub transcends the traditional, discipline-based organizational structure of universities by providing a central location, or hub, where students, faculty, and staff from all departments can collaborate. The following colleges, departments, schools and programs have all contributed significantly to the success of the Innovation Hub.

Services

Our student ambassadors and staff have the expertise to assist with any design, technology, or fabrication need.

 

Education
Discover your inner innovator!

Onsite and online education and support in design, innovation and new technologies through classes, workshops, demonstrations, and training programs from beginner to expert level.
