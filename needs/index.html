Needs Hub Purpose


The innovation and entrepreneurship community at Florida State University would like to know about needs, problems, or issues that you face or have observed. Our student entrepreneurs want to tackle them with or for you!

The Needs Hub is a web application for reviewing, creating, maintaining, and collaborating on need statements. Please add a need statement by completing the form in the SUBMIT A NEED tab. Feel free to submit multiple needs. We won't contact you unless you indicate your willingness to be contacted.


Thank you!

HeroX

Click here to visit herox.com

HeroX was co-founded in 2013 by XPRIZE founder Peter Diamandis, challenge designer Emily Fowler and entrepreneur Christian Cotichini as a means to democratize the innovation model of XPRIZE.

HeroX exists to enable anyone, anywhere in the world, to create a challenge that addresses any problem or opportunity, build a community around that challenge and activate the circumstances that can lead to a breakthrough innovation.

Oxford Global Challenge

Click here to visit oxfordglobalchallenge.com

The Global Challenge is a chance for students and recent graduates to learn more about the issues they care about and present their findings to the world.

Tackling global challenges starts with understanding a problem and its wider context, rather than jumping straight into a business plan or an idea for a quick fix. Participants are asked to demonstrate a deep understanding of a pressing social or environmental issue by mapping out the landscape of the current solutions and identifying missing opportunities for positive change.
